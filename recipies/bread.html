Bread Recipe

Description

This bread machine recipe is easy and foolproof. It makes a very soft and tasty loaf of bread with a flaky crust.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 package bread machine yeast
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
